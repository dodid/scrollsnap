1. Data we process
ScrollSnap processes videos you choose and generates images from them.

2. Storage
Generated images are stored locally on your device in the app library.

3. Photos access
If you allow permission, the app can import videos from Photos and save output images to Photos.

4. Sharing
Images are only shared when you explicitly use the share action.

5. Analytics and tracking
This app does not require personal account data to work.

6. Your control
You can delete generated images from the app library at any time.

7. Changes
We may update this policy as the app evolves.
